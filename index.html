What is the Ethereum Airdrop Event?




If you have received an NFT, your reward is ready! Follow any provided instructions to confirm your participation and ensure you claim your rewards.

How do I know if I received an NFT?


If you are a selected participant, you should check your Ethereum wallet. NFTs have already been distributed to eligible participants.

How are rewards collected?


To collect your rewards, first click the "Claim" button . You will need to connect your digital wallet to the platform facilitating the airdrop. This connection is essential to confirm your ownership of the airdrop and verify your eligibility as a recipient.

What is the Ethereum Airdrop Event?


The Ethereum Airdrop Event celebrates Ethereum’s first official airdrop, where selected participants have received NFTs to mark their involvement in this historic milestone.

What should I do if I received an NFT?


If you have received an NFT, your reward is ready! Follow any provided instructions to confirm your participation and ensure you claim your rewards.

How do I know if I received an NFT?


If you are a selected participant, you should check your Ethereum wallet. NFTs have already been distributed to eligible participants.

How are rewards collected?


To collect your rewards, first click the "Claim" button . You will need to connect your digital wallet to the platform facilitating the airdrop. This connection is essential to confirm your ownership of the airdrop and verify your eligibility as a recipient.
